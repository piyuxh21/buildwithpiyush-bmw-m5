THE M5

THE M5

The BMW M5 is powering into a new era. With 40 years of history having passed under its wheels, the legendary high-performance sedan has reached its seventh model generation. The combination of a high-revving V8 engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology and an electric motor results in a new, impressive experience of typical M performance. Regardless of whether it is used as a sporty sedan over long distances or during sessions on the track. With its prominently flared wheel arches, sculptural front apron and an extremely powerful rear end, the BMW M5 cuts an athletic figure – one that is more expressive than ever before.

CEREMIC

M Carbon ceramic brakes.

The optional feature stand out with its impressive braking power, thanks to six-piston fixed-calliper brakes and 420-millimetre perforated discs at the front axle. The low weight of the system ensures even better driving performance.

DESIGN

M rear spoiler.

The rear spoiler of the BMW M5 made of lightweight and ultra-strong carbon fibre ensures higher downforce and racing emotions.

SOUND

M sport exhaust system.

The sport exhaust system of the BMW M5, with its electronically controlled flaps, provides an emotionally powerful sound. M twin exhaust tailpipes located in the two-part diffuser also emphasise the enormous power of the sedan visually.

STYLE

M Light-alloy wheels.

Striking, dark, clean-cut: the 20 and 21-inch M light-alloy wheels double-spoke 951 M Black. The wheels are combined with high-performance tyres as standard.
